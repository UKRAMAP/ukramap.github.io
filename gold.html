Gold quick summary:

We have yet to run a formal gold project, so this is very much an idea rather than a plan. Any volunteers are very welcome. Here are some things to think about when approaching a gold project:

  • This is rocket project, concentrate on flying
  • As each project is different, so you may complete more than one Gold award
  • Prepare a plan we can have a look at
  • Expect this to be weeks of work

We have a template for a Gold project for anyone who is interested.

The hope for Gold projects is that flyers will learn new skills and apply them while being able to access guidance and knowledge from more experienced rocket flyers.
